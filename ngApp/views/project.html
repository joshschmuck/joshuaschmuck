Weather Share

Weather Share is a web application where users can share weather conditions in their current locations. The users can also see post from other users if they are hoping to find the perfect conditions.
This was an individual project that was required to finish school at Coder Camps.

Working Mothers Connection (WoMoCo)

Working Mothers Connection is a web application where working moms can connect with other working moms. Moms can connect, message each other, share resources, recomendations, and get together to keep the kids busy.
This was a group project where I was worked with a team of 5 others at Coder Camps. This project was a requirement to graduate.
